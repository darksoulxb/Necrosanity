# NecroSanity

**Your computer is falling apart from the inside.**

We are here to see how much life is left in it before it completely breaks down.

NecroSanity is a tool that checks how well your computer is working. It looks at how stable it's finds problems before they get bad and tells you how close your computer is to stopping work altogether.

### Features

- Shows you how well your computer is working in time. From barely working to completely broken

- It checks for problems like slow speed, memory issues and wasted resources

- Displays show you exactly how bad your computer is doing

- We tell you the truth about your computer no matter how bad it is.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

NecroSanity looks at your computer and says:

**"How longer can you keep going?"**

---

NecroSanity made for system administrators, developers and anyone who is tired of watching their computers break down without warning.
